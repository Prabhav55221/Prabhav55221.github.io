Hi! I am Prabhav. I am currently pursuing my Master's in CS (Thesis) with a specialization in Human Language Technologies from JHU. I am doing my graduate research at the Center for Language and Speech Processing. 

I am fortunate enough to be advised by <a href="https://www.cs.jhu.edu/~jason/">Prof. Jason Eisner</a>. Under his guidance, I am working on developing ActiveRubric - where we hope to release research to better inform LLM-guided annotation process with (a new approach to) active learning and feature acquisiton at inference time. I am also working with <a href="https://engineering.jhu.edu/faculty/jesus-villalba/">Prof. Jesus Villalba</a> on speaker diarization and LLM based post-hoc refinement in Multimodal Emotion Recognition.

Previously, I have worked under <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/kpsrana1/home">Prof. KPS Rana</a> and <a href="https://vineetkumar27.wordpress.com">Prof. Vineet Kumar</a> at the <a href="https://apclnsit.wordpress.com">APCL, NSIT</a> during my undergraduate degree, where I researched ways to make language and speech models more humanistic. I had the privilege of working with <a href="https://iiitd.ac.in/debarka">Prof. Debarka Sengupta</a> at the IIIT Delhi Speech Research Laboratory - Where we worked on a new approach to Question-Answering Systems.
