Hi! I am Prabhav. I am currently pursuing my Master's in CS (Thesis) with a specialization in Human Language Technologies from JHU. I am doing my graduate research at the Center for Language and Speech Processing under <a href="https://engineering.jhu.edu/faculty/jesus-villalba/">Prof. Jesus Villalba</a> in <a href="https://engineering.jhu.edu/faculty/najim-dehak/">Prof. Najim Dehak's</a> Lab.

I am fortunate enought to have worked under <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/kpsrana1/home">Prof. KPS Rana</a> and <a href="https://vineetkumar27.wordpress.com">Prof. Vineet Kumar</a> at the <a href="https://apclnsit.wordpress.com">APCL, NSIT</a> during my undergraduate degree, where I researched ways to make language and speech models more humanistic. I had the privilege of working with <a href="https://iiitd.ac.in/debarka">Prof. Debarka Sengupta</a> at the IIIT Delhi Speech Research Laboratory - Where we worked on a new approach to Question-Answering Systems.

I have done my best to describe my research below. Please feel free to reach out to me for any question/collaborations.
